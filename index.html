Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you connect third-party services such as Jira, Slack, Gmail, and other supported platforms.

Information We Collect

When you authorize the App, we may collect:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Your account identifier provided by the connected platform
  • Workspace or project identifiers
  • Metadata necessary to access your connected services
  • Content retrieved from connected services only after you explicitly authorize access

How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your connection to supported services.
  • Retrieve data that you authorize the App to access.
  • Provide connector functionality and related features.
  • Maintain and improve the service.

Data Storage

OAuth credentials are stored securely and are used only to access the services that you explicitly authorize.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except when required by law or when necessary to provide the requested service.

Revoking Access

You may revoke this application's access at any time through your connected service's account settings.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect your information against unauthorized access.
